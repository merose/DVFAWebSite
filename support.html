Support Del Valle Fine Arts

Del Valle Fine Arts relies on your generous contributions to help defray the costs of our concerts. Donations also help to fund our free tickets for youth and our school outreach programs.

Much of the work involved with presenting our concerts is done by unpaid volunteers. Nonetheless, artist and production fees are escalating, so we are counting on our patrons to help bridge the gap between our income from ticket sales and our actual expenses. Your donations will be greatly appreciated.

Contributor levels are:

Benefactor
$500 +
Patron
$250 – $499
Supporter
$100 – $249
Friend
$25 – $99

Contributors will be listed with appreciation in the concert program notes.

Joining Encore Society

When you remember Del Valle Fine Arts in your Will or Estate Plan, you help to ensure that quality solo and chamber music performances will always have a place in our community. Your generous gift will benefit countless others for years to come.

To make a gift through your Will or Trust, or to make a gift directly from your IRA or other retirement plan, please talk with an attorney knowledgeable in estate planning. You may make a gift of a specific amount of money or a percentage of your estate.
