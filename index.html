  • With special guest Dr. Sarah Waltz, Associate Professor of Music History

Del Valle Fine Arts presents five world class chamber music concerts each season at the beautiful Bankhead Theater in downtown Livermore, California. Tickets are available at the Bankhead Theater ticket office at 2400 First Street, Livermore, CA.

The box office is open Tuesday through Saturday from noon to 6:00 PM, and on performance days starting 2 hours prior to performance time. You can call the box office at 925.373.6800 or visit them on the web at livermorearts.org.
