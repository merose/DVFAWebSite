For Artists and Agents

If you are a presenter and would like to have your artist considered for presentation by Del Valle Fine Arts, please send material to:

Del Valle Fine Arts
P.O. Box 2335
Livermore, CA 94551

You can also send email to our submissions mailbox. Phone calls are less effective as we are an all volunteer organization without regular office hours.

Every year we decide what types of programs we will present in our next season. The material we have received, if appropriate, will be evaluated along with material sought out and received from other artists. You will be contacted if we need a CD or more information. Our selections are made based on CD recordings, PR materials and reports from local people who have attended concerts by the artists.

Thank you for your interest.
